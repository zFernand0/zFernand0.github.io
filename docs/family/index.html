About Our Family

We are the Rijo Suriel family, Fernando R.C. and Candy S.S.! We hail from the beautiful Dominican Republic and are passionate about our faith and the joy of being together.

Our life is an adventure, filled with memorable travels and the simple pleasures of spending quality time with each other. We believe in supporting each other, embracing new experiences, and creating lasting memories guided by our love for God.

This website is a place for us to share our journey, from our travels to our everyday joys. We hope you enjoy getting to know us better!
